The Wonders of Wanderlust

My name is Karlena and I am a 17 year old high school junior. Some of my favorite things to do are sing, play guitar, spend time with my church fam, and daydream. My wanderlust stemmed from watching Louis Cole on YouTube. He would document all of his amazing adventures around the world. By watching his vlogs, I was able to live vicariously through him and go all over the world. Seeing just how different every country is made me want to go and experience them for myself. I hope to study abroad while in college and eventually travel with a group of friends afterwards (I'll travel by myself if I have to, though!). Aside from that, my wanderlust was fed and grew even larger through my French classes. There were times where we would learn cultural tidbits of France and I would be so facinated by even the little things like "la bise" or how French dinner times ran well into the night. I then began to wonder what families in other countries did together. I thought to myself, "Why don't I just go out and experience it for myself?". And that's exactly what I plan to do. But for now, I'll have to keep wondering. I hope that this webite will help you to see the world in the same way that I do, even though I haven't experienced most of it yet. Maybe it'll inspire you to go some adventures of your own. Anyway, goodbye for now! -Karlena
